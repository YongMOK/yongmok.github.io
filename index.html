About me

Hello! I'm Yong MOK, a passionate 3rd year student at École Polytechnique, specializing in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence. Originally from Cambodia, I've been on an exciting journey pursuing excellence in mathematics, physics, and computer science.

My academic path led me from the Institute of Technology of Cambodia to one of France's most prestigious engineering schools, where I've been fortunate to receive the Eiffel Excellence Scholarship. This journey has shaped my perspective on the transformative power of technology and education.

I'm particularly passionate about machine learning, deep learning, and AI model robustness. Through my research work at CMAP, I've explored cutting-edge topics in high-dimensional model security, developing novel attack strategies that contribute to the field of AI safety.

Beyond academics, I'm an active member of various student organizations, including serving as President of the XKhmer Student Association. I also compete in boxing, where I earned a silver medal at Coupe de l'X, and enjoy volleyball, reading academic articles, and painting in my free time.

I believe in the power of education to transform lives and communities, which drives my passion for tutoring and volunteering. As I prepare to graduate in April 2025, I'm excited about contributing to the field of Data Science and AI while making a positive impact on the world.
